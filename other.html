IBT Management System


Summary

During my internship at DAF Trucks N.V. I had to create a management system for the ICT (Industrial Control Techniques) department called Intelligent Project Control and Reporting. Unlike other projects, I still have a lot of information and screenshots of this project. I will add these later on. In short, it's an order system where certain factories inside DAF can order assistance from the ICT department. This is probably one of the biggest software projects I have worked on. Sadly, they decided not to use it because of the inefficiency of the code. Which I understand due to me being new to Software development (only 1 year experience at that point).

Functionalities

  • Create account with different roles.
  • Fill in forms (Order assistance or evaluation).
  • View multiple overviews with a lot of information.
  • Keep track of KPI's (Key Performance Indicators)
  • Logs
  • Manual
  • Search functions
  • Admin role can view users and delete them
  • Orders have to be reviewed and accepted before they can be worked on.
  • Application gets and sets information from/to the database
